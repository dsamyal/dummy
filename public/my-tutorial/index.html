This sound sample was sent to us and we were asked if we could make it sound more ready for release. We tried to give suggestions to changes in composition and mixing. This tutorial series is aimed at beginners who struggle to get their music as they want it.

Artists can send in music clips and we analyze them and give our suggestions to how to improve it. This is absolutely free and everyone can send in their sample.

If you would like help with your music, either mixing, composition or mastering, we will try to give you suggestions to how you can get faster to the result you want to get.

Write us if you are interested in having your music analyzed. If you have certain issues you would like to get addressed, that is also possible. If you have a certain sound or production you would like to have, then you can just name the titles (please do not send us tracks where you do not own the rights).

With this tutorial series we will try to help upcoming musicians to get to their goal faster, and it is a part of the ARTfora art project. ARTfora is a forum for artists where artist can get help from other artists.
